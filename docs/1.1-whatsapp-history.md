## WhatsApp Coexistence â€” Full Research Summary

### What is it?

Meta's official name is **"Onboarding WhatsApp Business App users (aka Coexistence)"**. It allows a business to use **both** the WhatsApp Business App **and** the Cloud API simultaneously on the **same phone number**. Messages are mirrored between both apps.

**Doc reference:** https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/embedded-signup/onboarding-business-app-users

---

### Is it possible? YES

After connecting, AirysChat can:
1. **Sync the last 6 months of message history** from the WhatsApp Business App into AirysChat
2. **Sync all contacts** that have a WhatsApp number
3. **Mirror messages in real-time** â€” messages sent from the WhatsApp Business App appear in AirysChat and vice versa

---

### How it works (3-step sync)

| Step | API Call | What it does |
|------|---------|-------------|
| 1. Contacts sync | `POST /<PHONE_NUMBER_ID>/smb_app_data` with `sync_type: "smb_app_state_sync"` | Triggers `smb_app_state_sync` webhooks with all WhatsApp contacts |
| 2. History sync | `POST /<PHONE_NUMBER_ID>/smb_app_data` with `sync_type: "history"` | Triggers `history` webhooks with up to 180 days of message history (in 3 phases) |
| 3. Ongoing mirror | Subscribe to `smb_message_echoes` webhook | Every message sent from the WhatsApp Business App triggers a webhook so AirysChat can display it |

### Key Limitations

- **Throughput capped at 20 msg/sec** for coexistence numbers (vs. 80 default)
- **Group chats** are NOT synced
- **Disappearing messages, view once, live location** are disabled for 1:1 chats
- **Broadcast lists** become read-only
- **Voice/video calls** â€” not supported via API
- **Unsupported countries**: Nigeria, South Africa
- Only one sync attempt per onboarding (must offboard and re-onboard to resync)
- Media messages in history only include asset IDs for the last 14 days

---

### Webhooks AirysChat needs to handle

| Webhook Field | Purpose |
|---------------|---------|
| `history` | Historical message threads (up to 180 days), delivered in phases/chunks |
| `smb_app_state_sync` | Contact add/edit/remove events from the Business App |
| `smb_message_echoes` | Real-time messages sent by the business from the WhatsApp Business App |
| `account_update` | PARTNER_REMOVED event when business disconnects |
| `account_offboarded` | Business changes device or re-registers |
| `account_reconnected` | Business re-onboards after offboarding |

### History sync details

Messages arrive in **3 phases**:
- **Phase 0**: Day 0 â†’ Day 1 (most recent)
- **Phase 1**: Day 1 â†’ Day 90
- **Phase 2**: Day 90 â†’ Day 180

Each phase may have multiple **chunks** (ordered by `chunk_order`). A `progress: 100` means sync is complete. A single webhook can contain thousands of messages.

### Pricing impact

- Messages sent from the **WhatsApp Business App** remain **free**
- Messages sent via **Cloud API** (i.e., from AirysChat) follow normal Cloud API pricing
- Customer service window (24h) only applies to Cloud API messages, not Business App messages

---

### Bottom line

This is a very powerful feature for AirysChat customers who are **already using WhatsApp Business App** and want to graduate to AirysChat without losing their history or being forced to abandon the app. The business can continue using the WhatsApp Business App for quick one-off replies while AirysChat handles automation, templates, and team workflows.

The main blocker is that AirysChat needs to be registered as a **Meta Solution Partner or Tech Provider** to access this feature. Once that's done, the implementation is mostly about handling 3 new webhook types and building the Embedded Signup onboarding flow.
